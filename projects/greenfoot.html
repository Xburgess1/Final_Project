Information

This Greenfoot project was made for a Software Development course as a final project. The player plays as the ship on the bottom of the screen, shooting at the enemies at the top of the screen. The player can move side to side, shoot and fire a missile that is on a cool down. There are different types of enemies each with different properties. Some are just shields, some shot back at the player and the big boss tries to slam itself into you.
