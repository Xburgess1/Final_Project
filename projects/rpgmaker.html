Information

This pokemon game was made using RPG Maker XP and Pokemon Essentials. The goal is like any pokemon game, catch, train, battle, become the best. With RPG Maker XP I am able to create an entire custom world, with custom NPCs littered through it. I am also able to add new custom Pokemon, new moves for the Pokemon to learn and new abilities.
