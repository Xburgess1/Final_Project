Information

This project is a game I am creating using Unity, the game is being developed for mobile devices. The player plays as a triangle and competes in turn based combat with the enemy. The game starts off with your turn, the player can touch anywhere on the screen and pull back to launch the triangle, like a slingshot. As the player you want to ram into the enemies on your turn to destroy them while they will try to ram into you on thier turn. I am planning on adding more types of enemies with different ways of destroying you, and adding upgrades that the player will be able to buy with in game currency they collect by playing. I also want to add abilities the player can unlock to add new ways of destroying enemies.
